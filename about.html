ABOUT

Hello, I’m Motshwane Letsoalo, a food photographer based in Johannesburg, South Africa. I shoot with a bold, high-key aesthetic that celebrates the richness of our culture and the stories hidden in every meal.


Food is more than sustenance, it is heritage, connection, and memory. My lens chases that feeling: the warmth of a shared plate, the colour of our markets, the pride of recipes passed down through generations.
